FAQs

What is STS?

Skill Tracer System is a web application for tracking the skill evaluation of team members.

What is the app areas?

Main application areas of this solution are project managers in the business world. Forms are used for collecting the skills of people working on project. It can be integrated into job search and career sites.

What is the objective of this system?

This project has been developed to guide staffs to the right projects and to evaluate staffs on a project and to measure their capabilities. Staffs will enter their skills, then project managers will be able to select the staff who will work in their projects. As people develop themselves, their previously entered abilities will be dynamically updated.

Can I use the system?

System is designed for everyone.
